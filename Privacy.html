Privacy

Privacy on nRF Connect

nRF Connect is a standalone app. The app does not collect any personal information, location, device information.

Data Usage

Communication between your phone or tablet and peripheral are saved only locally. You can browse inbound, outbound messages and app behaviours in log file.

Network Connections

nRF Connect download only necessary information to provide function that can work properly. You can stop the mobile network usage in Settings at anytime.
